Ice Cream Data Analysis

About the Project

This project focuses on analyzing ice cream sales data to uncover patterns in customer preferences. The data includes information on ice cream flavors, ratings, customer age groups, and seasonal trends.

We used data visualization tools to generate insights on which flavors are most popular, how customer ratings fluctuate, and the impact of different factors on sales performance.
